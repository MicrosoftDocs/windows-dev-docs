# App submission controls for MSIX app

Submission controls let you manage your app submission more easily. You can delete a draft submission or cancel certification if you need to.

### Delete draft submissions and apps

To delete a draft submission, follow these steps:
1. Go to the Apps and Games overview page and open the app you want to delete. You can only delete an app that is not submitted or live on the Store.
2. From the **Product submission** (or product update card in case of an update submission) card, click **Delete submission**.
3. Confirm that you want to delete the app submission and all its information.

If there are no remaining draft submissions, you can delete your app.
1. Go to the app overview page for the app you want to delete.
2. Beside the app name click on 3 dots and then click **Delete product**.
3. Confirm that you want to delete the app.

### Cancel certification

To cancel certification, follow these steps:
1. Go to the Apps and Games overview page and open the app you submitted for review or certification.
2. Navigate to the Application overview page for your app.
3. From the **Certification status** card under Product release section, click on 3 dots on the far right side and then click on **Cancel certification**.
4. Confirm that you want to cancel the review process.
