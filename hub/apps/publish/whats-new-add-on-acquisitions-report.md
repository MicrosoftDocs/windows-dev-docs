# Add-on acquisitions report

The [**Add-on acquisitions**](https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard/insights/analytics/store/inapp) report provides insights into add-on performance for your app or game. It shows how many add-on purchases are made and the revenue generated across subscription, durable, and consumable add-ons. 

Use this report to monitor purchase volume, track revenue, and evaluate add-on performance over time. Use filters such as date range and markets to refine the data.

## Overview 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-01.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Overview tab." lightbox="images/app-add-on-01.png":::

The **Overview** tab provides a summary of your app or game's add-on performance, including purchase and revenue insights. It includes key metrics, trends, and geographic distribution across subscription, durable, and consumable add-ons. 

Use this tab to quickly understand overall add-on performance over a selected time period. 

### Key metrics 

The **Key metrics** section shows a high-level summary of add-on purchases and revenue for the selected date range. 

* **Subscriptions purchased**: Shows the total number of subscription add-on purchases in the selected date range.
* **Consumables purchased**: Shows the total number of consumable add-on purchases in the selected date range.
* **Durables purchased**: Shows the total number of durable add-on purchases in the selected date range.
* **Total revenue**: Shows the gross revenue in USD from all add-on purchases (subscriptions, durables, and consumables) in the selected date range. 

### Trends 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-02.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Overview trends." lightbox="images/app-add-on-02.png":::

The **Trends** section shows how add-on purchases and revenue change over time. 

* **Purchase count by type**: Shows the number of purchases over the selected date range, broken down by add-on type (for example, subscriptions and durables).
* **Revenue by type (USD)**: Shows the gross revenue in USD over the selected date range, broken down by add-on type. 

### Geographical spread 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-03.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Overview tab geographical spread." lightbox="images/app-add-on-03.png":::

The **Geographical spread** section shows purchase counts by country or region. 

For each country or region, you can view: 

* Subscription purchases
* Durable purchases
* Consumable purchases 

This table helps you understand where your add-on purchases are coming from. 

### Conversions breakdown by campaign ID 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-04.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Overview tab conversions." lightbox="images/app-add-on-04.png":::

The **Conversions breakdown by campaign ID** section shows conversions and page views from customers on Windows 10 or Windows 11 for each custom campaign. 

## Subscriptions 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-05.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Subscriptions tab." lightbox="images/app-add-on-05.png":::

The **Subscriptions** tab provides detailed insights into subscription add-ons, including acquisition, revenue, and retention trends. It provides insights into subscription add-ons, including acquisition, revenue, and retention metrics. 

### Key metrics 

The **Key metrics** section summarizes subscription status and activity for the selected date range. 

* **Active subscriptions**: Shows the number of active non-trial subscriptions as of the latest available date.
* **Active trials**: Shows the number of active trial subscriptions as of the latest available date.
* **New subscriptions acquired**: Shows the total number of new subscription acquisitions in the selected date range. This includes trial subscriptions.
* **Churned**: Shows the total number of subscriptions that were canceled, expired, or refunded in the selected date range. 

### Subscription acquisitions 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-06.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Subscriptions tab acquisitions." lightbox="images/app-add-on-06.png":::

The **Subscription acquisitions** section shows the acquisition count for subscription add-ons. 

* Displays acquisition count per selected add-on over the selected date range
* Allows filtering by purchase status
* Allows selection of one or more add-ons for comparison 

### Subscription revenue (USD) 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-07.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Subscriptions tab revenue." lightbox="images/app-add-on-07.png":::

The **Subscription revenue (USD)** section shows revenue trends for subscription add-ons. 

* Displays the gross revenue in USD per selected add-on over the selected date range
* Allows selection of one or more add-ons to compare performance 

### Subscription details 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-08.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Subscriptions tab details." lightbox="images/app-add-on-08.png":::

The **Subscription details** section provides a breakdown of subscription metrics per add-on. 

* Shows totals for each subscription add-on over the selected date range
* Includes metrics such as active subscriptions, active trials, new acquisitions, and churn 

### Cohort retention 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-09.png" alt-text="Screenshot of cohort retention." lightbox="images/app-add-on-09.png":::

The **Cohort retention** section shows how groups of users who start a subscription in the same month are retained over time. 

* Each row represents a cohort of users who subscribed in a specific month
* Each column shows the percentage of that cohort retained in subsequent months 

You can filter the data by user groups: 

* **All users**: Includes all users who acquired a subscription
* **New users**: Includes users who acquired a subscription for the first time 

## Durable & Consumable 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-10.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Durable & consumable tab." lightbox="images/app-add-on-10.png":::

The **Durable & consumable** tab provides insights into durable and consumable add-ons, including their purchases and revenue. Use this tab to understand how durable and consumable add-ons perform over time and compare performance across add-ons. 

### Key metrics 

The **Key metrics** section summarizes purchase and revenue activity for durable and consumable add-ons. 

* **Durable purchased**: Shows the total number of durable add-on purchases in the selected date range.
* **Durable revenue**: Shows the gross revenue from durable add-on revenue in USD for the selected date range.
* **Consumable purchased**: Shows the total number of consumable add-on purchases in the selected date range.
* **Consumable revenue**: Shows the gross revenue from consumable add-on revenue in USD for the selected date range.  

### Trends 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-11.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Durable & consumable trends." lightbox="images/app-add-on-11.png":::

The **Trends** section shows purchases and revenue trends for durable and consumable add-ons over time. 

#### Consumable & durable purchase 

* Shows the purchase count per selected add-on over the selected date range
* Allows filtering by category
* Allows selection of one or more add-ons to compare 

#### Consumable & durable revenue (USD) 

* Shows the gross revenue in USD per selected add-on over the selected date range
* Allows filtering by category
* Allows selection of one or more add-ons to compare 

### Consumable & durable details 

:::image type="content" source="images/app-add-on-12.png" alt-text="Screenshot of Consumable & durable details." lightbox="images/app-add-on-12.png":::

The **Consumable & durable** details section provides a breakdown of add-on performance. 

* Shows totals per add-on (durables and consumables) over the selected date range
* Allows sorting by:
  * Quantity sold
  * Revenue 

> [!NOTE]
> Some sections and metrics may not display data if the corresponding add-on types (subscriptions, durables, or consumables) aren't configured or if there is insufficient activity during the selected date range.
